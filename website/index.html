The Story Behind Every Stitch

Behind every stitch is
a lifetime of skill.

Our Dadis aren't just employees — they are artists. Women who have carried this ancient craft in their hands for 30, 40, even 50 years, stitching stories into every thread.

When you buy from Dadi's Stitches, you're directly funding fair wages for women who deserve dignity and purpose — not just in their youth, but in the golden years too.

"I have been stitching since I was 12 years old. Now at 67, I stitch for families I'll never meet. That makes my heart full."
— Savitri Dadi, Mumbai · 55 years of embroidery

How It's Made

From your heart to their hands —
here's how the magic happens

1

You Choose

Pick from our curated collection or describe your dream design. We'll help you find the perfect shirt and embroidery style.

2

We Match Your Dadi

Based on your design, we match you with the Dadi whose specialty is closest to your vision. It's a partnership built on skill.

3

She Stitches (~4 Hours)

Your Dadi begins. Every design takes a minimum of 4 hours of careful, patient work. This is not rushed. This is love, at its own pace.

4

Shipped with Love

Packed by hand, with a personal note from your Dadi. Delivered anywhere in India in 5–7 days. Free shipping over ₹2,500.
