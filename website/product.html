Savitri Dadi's Story Savitri began embroidering at age 12, learning from her own grandmother in a small village in Maharashtra. Now at 67, she has raised three children and stitched thousands of designs — but her favourites are always sunflowers. "They remind me of mornings," she says. This shirt took her 4 hours and 23 minutes. Every petal was placed with care. Every stem was drawn freehand.
